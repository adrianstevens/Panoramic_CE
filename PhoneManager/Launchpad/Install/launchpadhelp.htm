Overview

Panoramic LaunchPad allows you the freedom to easily access your Windows Mobile applications, settings, contacts, files and folders with your finger tips. Flip between multiple customizable favorite screens for quick access to your most frequently used items. An easy to use touch kinetic scroll menu allows you to find the Program or device Setting you are looking for in seconds.

How To Use

To navigate between favorites, programs and settings, either tap on the desired screen button or drag the menu tab. Tapping on the selected menu tab at anytime will pop up the main menu allowing access to the options, help, or about screens.

Favorites Screen

The Favorites screen will appear when you first open Launch Pad. To add a shortcut to this screen simply select the "+" icon/button in an empty slot. A popup menu will appear allowing you to choose and insert the desired item. You can move a program/setting/contact/file/folder to any empty slot by selecting and holding down on the icon (for one second) and dragging it to the desired location with your finger. Tapping an icon will launch the desired shortcut. To remove a shortcut simply tap and hold the icon (for one second) and drag it on top of the recycle bin icon (lower right). Switch quickly between favorites screens by swiping left or right on the screen.

Programs Screen

The Programs Screen will display your device programs from A to Z, listing all folders at the top of the screen. Scroll through the Programs kinetic list quickly by using your finger or stylus. By tapping or pressing the d-pad on a selected program you will launch that program.

Settings Screen

By default the Settings Screen will display your device personal,settings, and connections from A to Z. Scroll through the kinetic list quickly by using your finger or stylus. By tapping or pressing the d-pad on a selected setting you will launch that screen.

Options

Access the options menu by simply tapping the selected toolbar tab to reveal a pop up menu. Choose options.

General - Color scheme: allows you to change the skin color.

General - Background: allows you to select your desired background.

General - Use in Fullscreen: Set Launchpad as a full screen app or not.

General - Start menu: choose "On" or "Off" to have the start menu launch the LaunchPad program.

General - Set today screen menu: choose "On" or "Off" to set LaunchPad at the bottom of your today screen.

General - Set start menu icon: choose between "Default", "Windows", or "Rocket" to change the icon displayed at the top left start menu.

Favorites - Icon size: choose between "Large","Normal", or "Small" to change the icon size on the Favorites screen.

Favorites - Draw tiles: Draw the background tiles on the Favorites screen.

Favorites - Show name: choose "On" or "Off" to display names on shortcuts on the Favorites screeen.

Uninstalling

To uninstall from your Windows Mobile Pocket PC: (WM Classic/WM Professional)

  • Go to Start | Settings | System tab | Remove Programs.
  • Select on the name of the Panoramic game or application and tap "Remove".
  • Follow the onscreen instructions.

Your Panoramic Windows Mobile application should now have been uninstalled.

Note, when updating your Panoramic Software, you should uninstall any old versions before installing the new version. In most cases, you can install a new trial version to update an older full version unless the new version is a paid upgrade.
