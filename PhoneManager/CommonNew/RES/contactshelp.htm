Overview

Panoramic Contacts Manager allows you the freedom to easily manager your contacts on your Windows Mobile device. Flip between two customizable favorites screen for quick access to your most frequently used contacts. An easy to use touch scroll menu allows you to find the person you are looking for in seconds. Sort your contacts by first, last name or company.

How To Use

After installing the Panoramic Contacts Manager simply use your device's Contacts button to begin.

Favorites Screen

The Favorites screen will appear first. To add contacts to this screen simply select the "+" icon/button in an empty slot. The contacts menu will appear allowing you to choose the desired contact. You can move the contact to any empty slot by selecting and holding down the contact and dragging it there with your finger.

Contacts Screen

By default the Contacts Screen will display your contacts from A to Z. To change the sorting select the Contacts menu button, another menu will appear choose Options. Within the Options Screen you can choose between sorting by First Name, Last Name or by Company. Any contact that does not fit into these categories is displayed last. Scroll through the Contact list quickly by using your finger to touch the A-Z alphabet to the right of the list. When selected, a Contacts information will be displayed in an expanded box.

History Screen

The History Screen allows you to view and sort all of your calls, emails and sms messages.

Contact Details Screen

This screen displays a Contacts phone numbers, email, and SMS information. By selecting one of these you can call, email or sms message a contact instantly.

Options

Within the Options Screen you can choose between sorting by First Name, Last Name or by Company.

Uninstalling

To uninstall from your Windows Mobile Pocket PC: (WM Classic/WM Professional)

  • Go to Start | Settings | System tab | Remove Programs.
  • Select on the name of the Panoramic game or application and tap "Remove".
  • Follow the onscreen instructions.

To uninstall from your Windows Mobile Smartphone: (WM Standard)

  • Go to Start | Settings | Remove Programs.
  • Select on the name of the Panoramic game or application and tap "Remove".
  • Follow the onscreen instructions.

Your Panoramic Windows Mobile application should now have been uninstalled.

Note, when updating your Panoramic Software, you should uninstall any old versions before installing the new version. In most cases, you can install a new trial version to update an older full version unless the new version is a paid upgrade.
