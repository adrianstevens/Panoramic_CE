Overview

Panoramic Contacts Genius allows you the freedom to easily manage your Windows Mobile contacts with your finger tip. Flip between two customizable favorites screen for quick access to your most frequently used contacts. An easy to use touch kinetic scroll menu allows you to find the person you are looking for in seconds. Check and sort your phone contact's call history with ease.

How To Use

After installing Panoramic Contact Genius, simply use your device's Contacts button to launch the favorites screen. To navigate between favorites, contacts, and history, either tap on the desired screen button or drag the black menu tab. Tapping on the selected menu tab (black) at anytime will pop up the main menu allowing access to the options, help doc, or about screen.

Contact Genius

Favorites Screen

The Favorites screen will appear first. To add contacts to this screen simply select the "+" icon/button in an empty slot. The contacts menu will appear allowing you to choose and insert the desired contact. You can move the contact to any empty slot by selecting and holding down the contact and dragging it to the desired location with your finger. Tap the contact to display their details. To remove a contact simply tap the favorites tab to reveal a pop up menu and choose "Remove Contact". Choose "Take picture of contact" in the menu to use your device camera and replace the existing contact image. Switch between 'Personal' and 'Business' contacts by using the slider at the top of the screen.

Contacts Screen

By default the Contacts Screen will display your contacts by last name from A to Z. To change the sorting select the "Contacts" menu button, another menu will appear choose "Options". Within the Options Screen you can choose between sorting by First Name, Last Name, Email or by Company Name . Any contact that does not fit into these categories is displayed last. Scroll through the Contact list quickly by touching your finger or stylus to the A-Z alphabet to the right of the list. When selected, a Contact's information will be displayed in an expanded box. By tapping or pressing the d-pad on a selected contact you will transition to the Details screen.

Contact Details Screen

This screen displays a Contacts phone numbers, email, and SMS information. By selecting one of these you can call, email or SMS message a contact instantly. By choosing the "menu" softkey you can edit a contact or view all recent conversation history. Choose the "back" softkey to return to the previous screen.

History Screen

The History Screen allows you to view and sort all of your calls. By choosing an item you can get a detailed view of the call. Tap the "history" menu tab (black) to reveal a pop up menu. Sorting options are located in the "options" menu. By choosing "Conversation history" when selected on an item you will display all recent calls from that person.

Options

Access the options menu by simply tapping the selected toolbar tab to reveal a pop up menu. Choose options.

General, Color scheme: allows you to change the skin color.

General, Replace default contacts app: choose between "On" or "Off".

General, Set today screen menu: sets the softkey to "Favorites" on your today/home screen.

General, Show animations: speeds up your device by turning off animations.

Favorites, Always show name: displays the contacts name on their picture.

Favorites, Picture size: choose between "Large", Normal (default)", or "Small" picture size.

Favorites, Left slider title: toggle between "Personal", "Business", "Friends", "Family", or "Favorites"

Favorites, Right slider title: toggle between "Personal", "Business", "Friends", "Family", or "Favorites"

Contacts, Sort contact list by: choose between first name, last name, email or company name .

Contacts, Show SIM contacts: choose between "On" or "Off".

Contacts, Show contacts detail mode: choose between "On" or "Off".

History, Show history: allows the user to choose how many items are displayed on the history screen.

History, Show call history: allows the user to show all, incoming, outgoing, missed calls on the history screen.

Uninstalling

To uninstall from your Windows Mobile Pocket PC: (WM Classic/WM Professional)

  • Go to Start | Settings | System tab | Remove Programs.
  • Select on the name of the Panoramic game or application and tap "Remove".
  • Follow the onscreen instructions.

Your Panoramic Windows Mobile application should now have been uninstalled.

Note, when updating your Panoramic Software, you should uninstall any old versions before installing the new version. In most cases, you can install a new trial version to update an older full version unless the new version is a paid upgrade.
